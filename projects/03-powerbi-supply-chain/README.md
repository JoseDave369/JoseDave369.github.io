## 📋 Project Overview

Comprehensive Supply Chain management dashboard built in Power BI to provide end-to-end visibility across logistics operations. Enables operations managers to monitor inventory, track supplier performance, and identify bottlenecks in real time.

## 📊 Dashboard Modules

- **Inventory Management:** Stock levels, reorder points, stockout risk
- **Supplier Performance:** On-time delivery rates, quality scores
- **Logistics KPIs:** Lead times, delivery accuracy, freight costs
- **Demand Forecasting:** Rolling trend analysis with time-intelligence DAX

## 🔑 Key Features

- Dynamic slicers for product category, supplier, and time period
- Conditional formatting to highlight critical thresholds (red/amber/green)
- Time-intelligence DAX functions: `DATEADD`, `TOTALYTD`, `SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR`
- Drill-through pages for supplier-level and product-level detail
- Custom tooltips with contextual KPIs
